About Jamhuuriya University

Our History

Jamhuuriya University was founded in 1998 with a mission to provide high-quality education and contribute to the development of Somalia. Over the years, the university has grown to become one of the leading institutions in the region, offering diverse programs and fostering research and innovation.

Our Mission

To educate and empower students to become knowledgeable, responsible, and innovative leaders who contribute positively to society locally and globally.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier university that inspires academic excellence, fosters creativity, and promotes sustainable development.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, ethics, and fairness in all our actions.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
  • Inclusivity: Creating a welcoming environment for all students and staff.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and critical thinking.
  • Community: Engaging with and serving the wider community.
